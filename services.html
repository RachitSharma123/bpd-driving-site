Government program

Free Learner Driving Lesson

If you're an eligible Victorian learner driver aged 16–19, you may be able to access a free professional driving lesson through the government's myLearnersDrive program. It's designed to give young learners access to a qualified instructor early in their learning journey.

This isn't a shorter or reduced lesson — it's a full, structured session with a qualified instructor who understands what young learners need at the start of their L plate journey.

  • Eligible learner drivers aged 16–19
  • Full session with a qualified instructor
  • Supervising driver participation included
  • Calm, structured approach for first-time learners
  • Program run through VicRoads eligibility

Driving Test Preparation

You've done the hours. Now it's time to make sure they count. Test preparation lessons focus specifically on the skills and behaviours assessed during your driving test — so nothing catches you off guard on the day.

This includes a full mock assessment run in test conditions, debrief on any weak points, practice on the test route, and targeted coaching on the specific things examiners mark students down for.

  • Full mock test in test conditions
  • Test route practice and familiarity
  • Targeted feedback on common fail points
  • Examiner expectations explained
  • Confidence building for test day
